Do's

1)Think before you post —
Always review your content for appropriateness before sharing it publicly.

2)Use privacy settings — 
Regularly update your account privacy settings to control who sees your content.

3)Verify information before sharing —
Always fact-check news or articles to avoid spreading misinformation.

4)Maintain a positive digital footprint — 
Share content that reflects your values and academic achievements professionally.

5)Take regular digital breaks — 
Limit screen time to protect your mental health and maintain academic focus.



Don'ts

1)Don't share personal information — 
Avoid disclosing your address, phone number, or daily routine with strangers online.

2)Don't engage in cyberbullying — 
Avoid posting hurtful, offensive, or disrespectful comments about others.

3)Don't overshare emotional content — 
Avoid posting in anger or distress, as it may negatively affect your reputation.

4)Don't plagiarize or share copyrighted content — 
Always give proper credit to the original creator.

5)Don't neglect your studies — 
Avoid excessive social media use during study hours or before deadlines.
